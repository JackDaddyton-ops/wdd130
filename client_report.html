Client Report

Client Report:

  • How did you decide what content and images to use for your site plan?

    • I wanted a website about the pomodoro study method which is named after pomodoro timers. They are like egg timers except they are shaped like tomatos instead of eggs. Pomodoro is Italian for egg, So I chose images and colors that remind me of Italian food.

  • How did you communicate with your developer? Was there any issues with communication?

    • When I originaly came up with the website idea, I thought that I would be developing it myself and had planned to vibe-code a pomodoro timer into it. The lead developer for my website emailed me about it, but before I could give them alternative content, they already took care of it and put the timer on one of the child pages. I also failed to communicate what resources to make links too. They were supposed to be links to YouTube videos to pomodoro timer videos and stuff.

  • Did your developer meet your expectations for your site?

    • Yes! For what I gave them to work with, They did a great job.

  • What will you do differently next time you hire a team to do a project for you?

    • I will put more energy into creating a good site plan that will be readable and make sense to people other than myself.

General Experience and Reflections:

  • What was the most challenging part of this project for you? Were you able to overcome it?

    • I think a lot of people in this class, including myself, struggled with communication. I think part of the reason, at least for me, was because it was hard to remember all the different projects, the other people involved in those projects, and their roles.

    • I think deploying a website you made that other people can see is really cool. I also liked seeing the changes different people made to the same projects I was a part of.

  • What would you do differently if you could do the project again?

    • I think I would write down each project I was involved in, and a list for each project of things I need to do(eg., people I need to email) in order to move forward with that project.

    • Yes. I think I have a clearer idea of what I need to do to communicate better with clients and team members.
